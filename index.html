Explore Heidelberg

Heidelberg is a town on the Neckar River in southwestern Germany. It’s known for venerable Heidelberg University, founded in the 14th century, the Gothic Heiliggeistkirche church towers over the cafe-lined Marktplatz and a town square in the Altstadt (Old Town). The red-sandstone ruins of Heidelberg Castle, a noted example of Renaissance architecture, stand on Königstuhl hill.
Even though it can be considered as one of the smaller cities in Germany there are always things to do in Heidelberg, no matter whether you like to explore restaurants and bars, cultural sites like museums or explore the beautiful nature surrounding Heidelberg. The city has a very studenty flair and is definitely worth a visit to explore the many cafes, bars and independent shops and boutiques.
